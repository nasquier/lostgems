LOST GEMS

Music by and for the ones who love it

Lost Gems is a free, non popularity-based social network that aims to bring together the different actors of music:

  • Composers
  • Players
  • Producers
  • Events organizers
  • Concert hall managers
  • ...and mostly listeners!

The goal is multifold:

  1. Get rid of the musical industry and the yoke of the star system

    by proposing something else than the disposable music imposed by majors on regular medias.



  2. Forging your critical sense

    by offering the first non popularity-based social network.



  3. Offering a better musical experience

    by suggesting things based on what you like and/or want to discover.



  4. Promoting

    local scenes, small labels and artists with limited resources. Encouraging live music !
